Welcome! I am **Shengtai Yao (姚圣泰)**, an incoming Ph.D. student in Operations Research within the Department of [Management Science and Engineering](https://msande.stanford.edu/) at [Stanford University](https://www.stanford.edu/). Previously, I obtained my Master’s degree in [Applied Mathematics and Statistics](https://engineering.jhu.edu/ams/) from [Johns Hopkins University](https://www.jhu.edu/), where I was fortunate to work with [Mateo Díaz](https://mateodd25.github.io/), [Luhao Zhang](https://luhao-zhang.github.io/) and [Yixuan Wu](http://yixuanwu.page/). Prior to this, I earned my Bachelor degree in [Mechanical Engineering](https://www.me.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/) (Elite program) from [Tsinghua University](https://www.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/) advised by [Qiang He](https://www.me.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/info/1265/2035.htm).

## Research Interest

Broadly speaking, I'm interested in ***Optimization and Machine Learning Theory*** and I'm currently working on topics related to Geometric Machine Learning.
